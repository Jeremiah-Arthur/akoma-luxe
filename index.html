The Scent Story

Fragrance
as Identity

Every Akoma Luxe product carries a deliberately composed fragrance journey rooted in West African botanicals. Our signature scent opens with the brightness of citrus and fresh moringa leaf — a nod to the living greenery of Ghana's landscape.

At the heart, warm floral amber notes bloom — drawing from the rich resin traditions of the Sahel. The base settles into Teak Bamboo and Woody Amber, a grounding finish that lingers gently on the skin long after rinsing.

Our fragrance is formulated using the Michael Edwards Fragrance Wheel (Woody Amber family) and complies fully with IFRA standards for rinse-off products.

✓ IFRA Category 9 Certified — Max 8.1% Fragrance Load — Rinse-Off Safe

Safety & Standards

IFRA Compliant.
Professionally Made.

Every Akoma Luxe product is formulated to meet the standards of the International Fragrance Association (IFRA). As rinse-off products, our liquid soap and bar soap fall under IFRA Category 9, with a maximum fragrance load of 8.1%. Our Teak Bamboo fragrance oil is certified compliant under the IFRA 47th Amendment, ensuring safety for all skin types.
